ABOUT WhatzUP App

WhatzUp is a relunch of E-Social app, to connects you on a latest upcoming events closest to you on a real time.

WhatzUp App is inspired by Philip Odunwo-Albert, to bring to you Upcoming events both local and global closer to you and many amazing social features, such as sharing photos, friends, facebook login, real-time chat, and many more.

The app is suitable for everyone who's personate on whats happening around now, funs minded person, holiday lovers, social, friend making and many more activities to your finger tips.


WhatzUp Features

  • Creating upcoming event in a Real Time and let friends/family add comments as it happen
  • Share your moment photo and let friends/family add comments
  • Facebook Auth Login
  • Crop and Share Photo
  • Friends followers / following
  • Chat in Real Time
  • Real Time Push Notification to followers
  • Easy ticket booking and seat reservation
  • With many more features you can exploit
